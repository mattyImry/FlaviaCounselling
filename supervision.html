Clinical Supervision

I have several yearsâ€™ experience providing clinical supervision both in my private practice and NHS work. I have supervised qualified and trainee clinical and counselling psychologists, CBT therapist and assistant psychologists. My approach to supervision is integrative and I draw from Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), especially third wave approaches such as Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Compassion- Focused Therapy (CFT) as well as psychodynamic ideas and Dynamic Interpersonal Therapy (DIT).

Thinking about interpersonal trauma and relationship dynamics, including the therapeutic relationship is a key part of supervision, especially with more complex cases. I am to create a non-judgemental space where supervisees feel free to discuss issues they are facing in client work, their own process and discuss other professional issues to support them to be the best therapist they can be.
